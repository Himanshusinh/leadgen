Phase 1 — MVP (2–4 weeks): "Find & export leads"

Auth + campaign creation form (industry + country/state/city filters)
Integrate one data source (Google Places API)
Show results in a table, dedupe, export CSV
This alone is sellable.
Phase 2 — Enrich & verify (2–3 weeks)

Add email finder (Hunter) + email verifier (NeverBounce)
Lead scoring (verified email + phone + website = higher score)
Phase 3 — Outreach & follow-ups (3–4 weeks)

Connect email sending (SES/SendGrid)
Sequence builder: step → delay → follow-up (scheduled via queue)
Track opens/clicks/replies (tracking pixel + reply detection)
Phase 4 — CRM & analytics (2–3 weeks)

Pipeline (Kanban: New → Contacted → Interested → Won)
Dashboard metrics
Auto-move leads on reply
Phase 5 — Intent leads (the "real buyers")

Landing-page form capture, Google/Meta lead-ad integration
This is where you get people who actually want the service.
